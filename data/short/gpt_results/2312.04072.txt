This paper outlines the development of a voice recognition robot, focusing on the technical components, programming concept, design architecture, and hardware implementation. The robot incorporates an Android application for voice commands, Bluetooth connectivity, and an ultrasonic sensor for obstacle detection and avoidance. It also utilizes Arduino microcontroller, motor drivers, relay modules, and PCB design. The paper concludes with the key findings and implications of the study, as well as potential future work in the field. Overall, the study presents a comprehensive framework for the development of voice recognition robots and their real-time surveillance and automation concepts.
